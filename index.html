A Reference to Money & Markets

Here to help you understand your money.

Most of us work hard, real-hard and saved! Only to realize that it's even more harder to manage money - just because we've never been taught how.

Our goal is to provide free education on Money in simple english and give real life corollaries. So you understand it as well as you understand object you use on a daily basis - like your pen or your car - and you are comfortable enough dealing with them.

And just as you won't sit in the car the first time and start driving it on the highway, please don't drive your money. Please use a practise account (paper money), drive it for a year and then get started with real money.
